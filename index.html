Vision Statement

Our Project

Pantry Pro is a place where people can create and save their own recipes. When creating recipes, Pantry Pro will allow users to add ingredients, steps, and a picture of the final product. While creating your own recipes is good, sometimes you might want to diversify your meals and find something new. This is why we have some default recipes available when you open the app and also why we are working on implementing recipe sharing with other users.

Our Users

Pantry Pro is designed for really anyone who cooks or wants to cook. It is a place to save recipes and plan meals so whether it's a professional cook wanting to try out new things and be able to easily change the recipe on the go or someone just trying to learn how to cook while staying organized in the kitchen, this app offers something of worth.

Criteria

We would consider this project a success if our users were able to:


Log in and get a familiar feel of the app. This would demonstrate that the platform is useable for people of all ages and abilities

Create their own recipes and be able to follow recipe's created by others. Adding ingredients and steps to follow to create a dish is the main goal of any good recipe. Share those recipes with other users/friends.


To measure this we will implement a survey to be answered by users who have used the app by a significant amount of time. The survey will evaluate the level of satisfaction perceived by the users at the aforementioned points.

We aim to have 90% of users reporting high satisfaction across the different points.

Create a personal profile

Create an account and log in with it

  • Press "Don't have an account? Sign up" to create an account
  • Fill in your info
  • Press "SIGN UP"
  • Login with your account after being redirected to the login screen
  • Press "LOG IN"

Search for recipes

Search by name or by type of food

  • Search by typing in keyword
  • Search by type of food (options bellow the search bar)

Create your recipe

Make your own post of your recipe

  • Go to create recipe tab [+]
  • Fill in title, duration, ingredients and instruction
  • Select picture of the recipe and its type of food
  • Press "Post your recipe" and it will show on top of the recipe list on homepage

Delete recipe you don't like

Remove the recipes that you don't want have it on the app

  • Select a recipe
  • Press option button on the top right of the screen
  • Press "Delete recipe"
  • Confirm your choice and it will no longer show on your homepage

Frequently Asked Questions

Is PantryPro free?

Yes, it is free. All users can use full functionalities of PantryPro without any associated fees.

Do we need an account to use the app?

Currently the app offers the same functionalities for all users, including guests. However, users with an account will be able to save their favourite reicpes and interact with recipe posts in upcoming update.
